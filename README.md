# BalanceAI

BalanceAI is an AI-assisted weekly planning and wellbeing assistant for university students.

It helps students turn fragmented academic, personal, social, work, and wellbeing commitments into a realistic weekly plan with overload warnings and explainable next-action recommendations.

## Core Promise

BalanceAI is not a generic calendar, therapy app, or life automation platform. Its MVP focuses on one practical promise:

- Build a realistic weekly plan from tasks, fixed commitments, check-ins, and preferences.
- Warn students before a day or week becomes overloaded.
- Recommend one next best action with a clear explanation.
- Suggest one suitable wellbeing or free-time activity using mock activity data.

## Target User

Busy university students aged 18-25 who are balancing classes, exams, group work, part-time work, family responsibilities, friendships, career goals, and wellbeing.

## MVP Features

- Onboarding questionnaire.
- Task and deadline input.
- Fixed commitment planner.
- Stress and energy check-in.
- AI-generated weekly schedule.
- Overload warnings.
- Next best action recommendation.
- Wellbeing/free-time suggestion.
- Explanation for every AI-generated recommendation.

## Guardrails

- No mental health diagnosis.
- No medical advice.
- No real booking, payment, or irreversible external action in v1.
- No real-time location tracking in the MVP.
- All AI recommendations must be explainable, editable, and dismissible.
